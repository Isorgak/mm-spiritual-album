About the Album

A Spiritual Journey of Faith

"I Trust in the Lord" is a spiritual music album performed by the St. Joseph La Salle Keren Choir in Eritrea. This collection represents the deep faith and devotion of the Catholic community in Keren.

Inspired by the prayerful life of the Church and the rich spiritual tradition of Eritrean Catholic communities, these songs carry powerful messages of faith, hope, mercy, and devotion.

The album invites listeners around the world to experience sacred music rooted in prayer and worship, connecting hearts across cultures through the universal language of spiritual song.

St. Joseph La Salle Keren Choir

The St. Joseph La Salle Keren Choir serves the Church through spiritual music and worship, sharing messages of faith and devotion through song. Based in Keren, Eritrea, the choir has been a cornerstone of the Catholic community's spiritual life.

Their music reflects the deep spiritual traditions of Eritrean Catholicism, blending ancient liturgical melodies with contemporary expressions of faith. Each performance is an offering of praise and a testament to their devotion.

About This Website

Website Organized and Produced by Mezgeb Mezmur Volunteers

This website was created and organized by volunteers from Mezgeb Mezmur to help share and preserve spiritual songs. The goal is to make these songs accessible to churches, choirs, and believers while supporting the long-term mission of preserving worship music.

The volunteers behind Mezgeb Mezmur have spent many years collecting, organizing, and documenting spiritual songs so they can be preserved for future generations.

Years of Dedication

Our Mission

To preserve, organize, and make accessible spiritual songs for churches, choirs, and believers around the world.

  • Preserve spiritual songs and traditions
  • Support church choirs and worship teams
  • Organize lyrics and recordings
  • Build a structured digital archive
  • Encourage global collaboration

Our Vision

A world where spiritual songs are preserved, organized, and easily accessible for all believers.

  • Create a trusted central archive
  • Connect believers globally through music
  • Ensure no song is lost to time
  • Support worship in every church
  • Inspire future generations

Mezgeb Mezmur Story

Since 2013

Mezgeb Mezmur began in 2013 when a small group of volunteers recognized a growing concern: many beautiful spiritual songs were at risk of being lost forever. These songs, passed down through generations in churches and communities, existed only in memory or scattered handwritten notes.

What started as a humble effort to collect and document these songs has grown into a comprehensive mission. Over the past 12 years, our volunteer network has expanded across regions, bringing together people who share a passion for preserving spiritual heritage.

Today, Mezgeb Mezmur stands as a testament to what dedicated volunteers can achieve. We've collected thousands of songs, built relationships with churches and choir leaders, and laid the groundwork for a digital platform that will make these resources accessible to believers worldwide.

"Our journey continues, and we invite you to be part of this meaningful mission."
